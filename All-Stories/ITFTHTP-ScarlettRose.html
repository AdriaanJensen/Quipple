It's time for the hero to pay

We won't be needed, when this is over we won't, but they'll count on one thing, even if we aren't, we'll always be there, even if they forget why we were there in the first place, because that's what we do, even if they don't appreciate it, that's what makes us different from the villains of this world.

They bring permanent change, for the valley is not built by strong winds, but by the firm resolve of steady waters. We are an extension of those principles, you may drop it whilst you are among the living, but remember this carefully for this is true, you will never destroy it, stamp it out no matter how hard you try, you will only wither and die if you do, and if you dare to destroy everything, what's a victor without his prize?

And if you wanted to, what's a victory without any witness?
